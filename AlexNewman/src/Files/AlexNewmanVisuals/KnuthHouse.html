Read below for a short biography of David Knuth

David Knuth was born on January 10th, 1938 and is considered one fo the grandfathers of Computer Science. He was born in America and is currently a professor at Standford University.
David Knuths most famous work called "The Art of Computer Programming",which is a seven volume work that was published from 1968-1973. In 1974 he won the Turing Award due to his book.
The books covered many parts of Computer Science but he won the award for their contribution to the analysis of algorithms.
Maybe even more famously though David Knuth used to give $2.56 for anyone that would find typing errors within his books, and this is because 256 pennies are 1 hexadecimal dollar.
David Knuth is still alive today and is still contributing to the growing world fo Computer Science.
